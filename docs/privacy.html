Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-03

Epoch Buddy is designed to work fully locally in your browser. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

Data collection

Epoch Buddy does not collect:

  • Personally identifiable information
  • Authentication credentials
  • Financial, health, or location data
  • Web history or browsing activity
  • Website content beyond your selected text for conversion

Data storage

The extension stores your last 10 conversions locally using the browser’s storage API. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time using the Clear History button.

Permissions

  • Storage: used to save recent conversion history.
  • Host access: enables selection-based conversion on any webpage.

Third-party services

Epoch Buddy does not use analytics, tracking, ads, or external servers.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, open an issue on our GitHub repository.
